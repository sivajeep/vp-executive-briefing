Leadership Philosophy

How I Lead.
How Organizations Win.

Technology doesn't support the strategy — it defines it. I build organizations where technology and business leadership are equal partners at the table, co-owning outcomes, not just enabling them.

In critical infrastructure — whether a payment network, an enterprise data platform, or an energy grid — failure has real-world consequences. I build cultures where resilience is the first principle, and teams are built to anticipate, absorb, and recover.
